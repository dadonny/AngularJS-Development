Onset of Effects: 45 mins - 2 hours

Peak Effects: 3 hours

Duration of Effects: 4 - 10 hours

Cannabis infused foods or liquids take extra time to be broken down because they pass through the gastrointestinal tract and liver before entering the bloodstream. Depending on how full a patient’s stomach is, it can take between 45 minutes and two hours to feel the effects, which last 6 to 8 hours.

When cannabis is ingested, a potent THC metabolite (11-hydroxy THC) forms in the liver, which is roughly 5 times more potent than the THC originally ingested. 11-hydroxy THC has a much more sedative effect as well, making edibles a popular choice for patients who have trouble sleeping.
